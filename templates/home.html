Worried about Parkinson's disease? Our innovative prediction tool empowers you with personalized insights into your potential risk.

Prediction using tremor and muscle rigidity

Parkinson's disease can cause tremors and muscle rigidity, which can manifest in a person's handwriting and drawing ability.

This app provides a preliminary assessment. For a confirmed diagnosis, please consult a healthcare professional.


Spiral Drawings:

People with PD may exhibit micrographia (smaller handwriting) and difficulty maintaining a consistent size and shape in spiral drawings. The model analyzes the uploaded image for these characteristics, which could indicate tremors or rigidity affecting fine motor skills.


Wave Drawings:

Similar to spirals, wave drawings created by people with PD might show irregularities in size, smoothness, and continuity due to tremors or stiffness affecting hand movements. The model assesses the wave drawing for such characteristics.



Prediction using MRI scans

Machine learning models can potentially analyze MRI scans to predict Parkinson's disease (PD) by identifying subtle changes in brain structure associated with the condition.

This app provides a preliminary assessment. For a confirmed diagnosis, please consult a healthcare professional.


Early Detection: Machine learning aided MRI analysis has the potential to detect Parkinson's disease earlier than traditional methods, allowing for earlier intervention and improved patient outcomes.
